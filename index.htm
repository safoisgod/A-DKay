Book Spotlight: Killed by Illiteracy

“Killed by Illiteracy” is a gripping exploration of how illiteracy shapes individuals and societies. Through poignant storytelling and real-life examples, the book unveils the harsh realities faced by those deprived of education.

How to Submit Your Manuscript

  • Prepare Your Manuscript

    Ensure your manuscript is formatted according to our guidelines. Use a standard font like Times New Roman, 12pt, double-spaced, with 1-inch margins. Include a title page with your contact information and a brief synopsis of your work.

  • Submit Your Manuscript

    Visit our submission portal and upload your manuscript in PDF or Word format. Fill out the required fields, including a cover letter and any additional materials. You’ll receive a confirmation email once your submission is complete.

  • Review & Feedback

    Our editorial team will review your manuscript within 6-8 weeks. You’ll receive detailed feedback on your submission, including suggestions for improvement and next steps if your work is selected for publication.
